effortless entry systems

State-of-the-art automatic door systems that provide smooth, reliable, and energy-efficient operation. From sliding glass doors to swing doors and revolving entries, our solutions combine advanced sensor technology with robust mechanical systems to deliver seamless access for commercial and residential applications.

intelligent sensors

Advanced motion detection and presence sensors that ensure smooth, safe operation for all users. Our intelligent sensor arrays can distinguish between approach intentions and casual proximity, preventing unnecessary door activations while ensuring responsive operation when needed.

  • Microwave motion sensors
  • Infrared presence detection
  • Safety beam protection
  • Adjustable sensitivity

adaptive operation

Variable speed control systems that adjust door operation based on traffic flow and user needs. Slower speeds for elderly or mobility-impaired users, faster operation during peak hours, and gentle closing to prevent accidents while maintaining efficiency.

  • Variable speed control
  • Traffic flow adaptation
  • Hold-open functionality
  • Emergency modes

energy efficiency

Energy-efficient operation with smart climate control integration. Minimal opening duration reduces energy loss while maintaining comfort. Integration with HVAC systems and building management platforms for optimal environmental control and cost savings.

  • Low energy consumption
  • Climate control integration
  • Standby power optimization
  • Environmental sensors
